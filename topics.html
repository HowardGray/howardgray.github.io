* Each Topic contains between 1 and 5 modules
* Each Topic is designed around a learning map. It lets you know what you should explore, in what order, and what is the broader theme you should be thinking about at each step. It also tells you how many weeks the whole thing will take.
* A Module takes 30-45 minutes to complete
* Modules are made up of a small number of Episodes, each taking 5-8 minutes * Episodes are created from a wide range of content and ideas, all curated by a collective of practitioners alongside the Fondo team
* As you progress through Modules, we'll provide different content to suit your learning style and goals
* Each Module is intended to be collaborative; partner up with a Learning Group or a Coach
* When you've completed a Module, you can add it to your Library and share your progress with others
